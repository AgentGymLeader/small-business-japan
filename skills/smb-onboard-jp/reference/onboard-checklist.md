# SMB Onboard JP Checklist

## Supported starting points

| Source | Useful for | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Accounting export | month-end, cash, accountant memo | CSV / XLSX / pasted table is enough for starter flow. |
| Bank CSV | cash timing, receipts, fixed costs | Do not infer tax treatment from bank memo alone. |
| Invoice PDF / register | overdue invoice review, accountant memo | Keep reminder drafts unsent. |
| Email thread | complaint, contract context, invoice follow-up | Quote only necessary excerpts. |
| CRM / spreadsheet | lead triage, customer list, campaign brief | No destructive merge in starter flow. |
| Contract PDF / DOCX | issue memo | No legal conclusion. |

## First workflow recommendation

| Owner pain | First safe workflow |
|---|---|
| "未入金がある" | Draft-only invoice review packet. |
| "月末締めたい" | Accountant question packet. |
| "契約書を見たい" | Contract issue memo. |
| "クレーム返信したい" | Response draft with approval checklist. |
| "採用したい" | Hiring checklist and sharoshi questions. |
| "何から始めればいい?" | Business context profile and first-file checklist. |

## Business context draft format

```markdown
## Business context

- Business type:
- Size:
- Main products / services:
- Customers:
- Tools / sources available:
- Professional advisors:
- Current top three pains:
- Preferred handoff format:
- Approval boundaries:
```

## Connector phrasing

Talk about the job unlocked, not the vendor's product pitch:

- Good: "会計exportがあると、月末の未分類や税理士への質問を整理できます。"
- Good: "Gmailがあると、督促文やクレーム返信の文脈が取りやすくなります。"
- Avoid: "freee is an accounting SaaS with..."

## Must not say

- A final tax, labor, legal, accounting, or filing conclusion.
- A claim that a qualified professional has already reviewed the output.
- A claim that a document is ready for government or professional submission.
- A claim that the assistant can send, file, or act on the owner's behalf.
- A claim that there are no legal, tax, labor, accounting, or filing issues.
- A claim of completeness or exhaustive coverage.
