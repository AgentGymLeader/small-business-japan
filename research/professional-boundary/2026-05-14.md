# Professional Boundary Memo

Date: 2026-05-14
Source refresh: official / quasi-official sources checked 2026-05-14
Scope: Small Business Japan public plugin boundary

## Design premise

The boundary is not "AI must not generate text." The boundary is that the plugin
must not perform or imply:

- representation.
- delegated agency.
- submission / filing.
- final professional determinations.
- professional-title display.
- autonomous external sending.

The plugin may prepare owner-reviewed drafts, checklists, issue memos, and
handoff packets when the owner remains the actor and qualified professionals are
escalated where appropriate.

| National Tax Agency warning, ["No.9204 にせ税理士にご注意"](https://www.nta.go.jp/taxes/shiraberu/taxanswer/osirase/9204.htm) | Avoid final tax calculations as advice, filing claims, and tax-professional representation. |

## Allowed

- Draft text for owner review.
- Evidence checklist.
- Missing-data list.
- Issue memo.
- Questions for accountant, lawyer, sharoshi, gyoseishoshi, or back-office
  partner.
- Comparison of user-provided data with explicit uncertainty.
- Reminder draft that is not sent.

## Caution

- Tax, consumption tax, withholding, payroll, social insurance, employment,
  permit, subsidy, contract, refund, collection, or complaint workflows.
- Any wording that sounds like completeness, official readiness, or professional
  review.
- Any tool connector that can write or send.

## Blocked

- Filing or submission.
- Final legal / tax / labor / accounting conclusion.
- Professional title impersonation.
- Automated payment, refund, send, post, CRM write, accounting write, or
  calendar modification.
- "Ready to submit" or "confirmed by professional" unless actually supplied by
  the user as a source.

## Forbidden label categories

- Claims that a tax accountant, labor and social insurance attorney,
  administrative scrivener, or lawyer has already confirmed the output.
- Claims that a document is ready for filing, submission, or application.
- Claims that there are no legal, tax, labor, accounting, or filing issues.
- Claims of completeness, exhaustiveness, or guaranteed detection.
- Claims that the assistant can send, file, apply, or act on the owner's behalf.

## README synchronization

The README must retain the plain boundary:

```text
This plugin helps prepare drafts, checklists, issue memos, and owner-reviewed
handoff packets. It does not provide financial, tax, legal, labor, HR, or
administrative-scrivener advice. It does not represent you, submit filings,
make final determinations, display professional credentials, or send anything
externally without your explicit approval.
```
