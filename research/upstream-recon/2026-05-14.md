## Starter layout decision

Use a separate draft directory:

```text
small-business-japan/
```

Reason:

- It keeps the localization readable as a standalone plugin while preserving the
  upstream Small Business mental model.
- It avoids changing upstream `small-business/` directly before maintainers
  express a preferred locale layout.
- If maintainers prefer `small-business/locales/jp/`, the tree can be moved in
  PAN-SBJ-070 without changing the output contracts.

## MCP / dependency decision

Reason:

  handoff target instead of being duplicated.
