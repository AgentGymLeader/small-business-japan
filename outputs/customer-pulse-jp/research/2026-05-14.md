# Research Dossier: customer-pulse-jp

Research date: 2026-05-14
Output: `customer-pulse-jp`

## Summary

Customer pulse summaries are safe as aggregate internal memos. They must not
resolve complaints, decide liability, complete APPI rights requests, or send
customer communications.

## Sources consulted

- PPC, [個人情報保護法ガイドライン 通則編](https://www.ppc.go.jp/personalinfo/legal/guidelines_tsusoku/), checked 2026-05-14.
- CAA, [特定商取引法ガイド 通信販売](https://www.no-trouble.caa.go.jp/what/mailorder/?r=freelancer), checked 2026-05-14.
- CAA, [通信販売広告Q&A](https://www.no-trouble.caa.go.jp/qa/advertising.html), checked 2026-05-14.

## Safe flags

`internal_memo_only`, `aggregate_minimize_pii`, `appi_use_purpose_check`,
`complaint_signal_not_resolution`, `no_auto_reply`.

## Forbidden claims

- APPI compliance or consent is complete.
- A complaint is legally invalid or resolved.
- Refund or liability status is final.
- Correction, deletion, disclosure, or third-party-provision-stop request is
  completed.

## Memo-only implications

Use aggregate themes and escalation candidates. Do not output send-ready
customer replies unless routed through a separate draft-and-approval workflow.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when APPI, CAA communication, or customer-dispute guidance changes.
