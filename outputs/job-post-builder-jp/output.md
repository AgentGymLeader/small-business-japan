# Output Contract: job-post-builder-jp

Status: proposed contract
Owner action boundary: read-only / draft-only / memo-only

## Mandatory output header

Every generated output must begin with:

```text
Small Business Japan by @agentgymleader. Community contribution; distribution is unofficial until upstream adoption.
Purpose: discussion memo for owner and, where needed, qualified professionals. Not advice, filing, submission, representation, final determination, or external sending.
目的: オーナーおよび必要に応じて担当士業と確認するための整理メモです。助言・申請・代行・最終判断・外部送信は行いません。
Research checked: 2026-05-14. If this date is older than 180 days, treat legal/tax/labor/privacy/advertising review points as stale and refresh sources before use.
```

## Purpose

Prepare a Japanese job-post draft and review checklist for a small business.
The output structures role facts, required working-condition items, fair-hiring
checks, missing data, and professional/owner review points. It does not make
hiring decisions or finalize employment terms.

## Inputs

- Role brief, draft job post, working-condition notes, salary/range memo,
  workplace/remote notes, contract-renewal notes, or pasted hiring context.
- Optional: company hiring policy, social-insurance/labor advisor instructions,
  screening process notes.

## Required output sections

1. `Summary`
2. `Job-post draft`
3. `Working-condition missing items`
4. `Fair-hiring review points`
5. `Personal-data collection review points`
6. `Questions for owner / labor advisor`
7. `Approval boundary`

## Japan compliance checks

- Include review candidates for 2024-04-01 working-condition disclosure changes:
  work location/task change scope, renewal cap, and indefinite-conversion
  opportunity/conditions where relevant.
- Flag age, sex, disability, family, birthplace, belief, union, health, and
  other sensitive or unfair-selection topics.
- Treat accommodation and applicant-data handling as review points.

## Negative scope

Do not claim legal compliance, discrimination removal, age/sex exception
validity, hiring fairness, social-insurance completion, or suitability of a
candidate. Do not publish job ads or collect applicant data.

## Approval pause

Stop before posting, sending, screening, collecting sensitive data, changing
employment terms, or submitting labor/social-insurance paperwork.

## Output format

Markdown draft and checklist only. Mark all job-post text as owner-review
draft.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when MHLW recruitment, working-condition disclosure, fair-hiring,
privacy, or labor-advisor guidance changes.
