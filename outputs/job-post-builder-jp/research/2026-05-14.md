# Research Dossier: job-post-builder-jp

Research date: 2026-05-14
Output: `job-post-builder-jp`

## Summary

Job-post generation is safe as a draft plus review checklist. It must not
finalize labor conditions, decide fair-hiring legality, collect unnecessary
sensitive data, or submit labor/social-insurance paperwork.

## Safe flags

`draft_only`, `working_conditions_check`, `fair_hiring_check`,
`sensitive_data_minimization`, `labor_advisor_review`.

## Forbidden claims

- The job post is legally compliant.
- Discrimination risk is removed.
- Age/sex exceptions are definitely allowed.
- AI can fairly decide hiring or candidate suitability.

## Memo-only implications

Use draft text plus review checklist. Keep final posting and employment terms
with the owner and, where needed, labor/social-insurance professionals.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when MHLW recruitment, working-condition disclosure, fair-hiring, or
privacy guidance changes.
