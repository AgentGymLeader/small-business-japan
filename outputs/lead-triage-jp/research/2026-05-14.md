# Research Dossier: lead-triage-jp

Research date: 2026-05-14
Output: `lead-triage-jp`

## Summary

Lead triage can rank contacts as an internal sales memo, but it must not replace
APPI, consent, opt-out, unsubscribe, or commercial-email review.

## Sources consulted

- PPC, [個人情報保護法ガイドライン 通則編](https://www.ppc.go.jp/personalinfo/legal/guidelines_tsusoku/), checked 2026-05-14.
- PPC, [オプトアウトによる第三者提供](https://www.ppc.go.jp/personalinfo/legal/optout/), checked 2026-05-14.
- DEKYO, [特定電子メール法のポイント](https://www.dekyo.or.jp/soudan/contents/taisaku/1-2.html), checked 2026-05-14.
- CAA, [特定商取引法ガイド 通信販売](https://www.no-trouble.caa.go.jp/what/mailorder/?r=freelancer), checked 2026-05-14.

## Safe flags

`memo_only`, `appi_use_purpose_check`, `acquisition_source_required`,
`no_auto_send`, `unsubscribe_check_required`, `third_party_provision_check`.

## Forbidden claims

- APPI compliance is complete.
- Marketing email can be sent.
- Public email addresses are freely usable.
- Consent or third-party provision permission is confirmed.

## Memo-only implications

Lead scores are internal sales candidates. Keep contact permission as
`要確認` and do not generate send-ready files.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when APPI, opt-out, unsubscribe, or commercial-email guidance changes.
