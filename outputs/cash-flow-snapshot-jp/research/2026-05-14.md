- This expense is deductible or this tax category is final.
- Consumption tax or input-tax-credit treatment is confirmed.
- Tax payable, filing status, or accountant-ready records are complete.
- A bank transfer, refund, or collection action should be executed.

Keep tax labels as unconfirmed review candidates.
