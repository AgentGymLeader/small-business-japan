# Research Dossier: month-end-prep-jp

Research date: 2026-05-14
Output: `month-end-prep-jp`

## Summary

Month-end preparation is safe as record organization and question generation.
It must not become final bookkeeping, journal approval, filing preparation, or
preservation-compliance certification.
