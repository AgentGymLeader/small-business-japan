# Research Dossier: contract-review-jp

Research date: 2026-05-14
Output: `contract-review-jp`

## Summary

Contract review should be issue spotting and handoff preparation. It must avoid
legal determinations, representation, negotiation, signing, and external send.

## Safe flags

`template_diff_only`, `general_clause_note`, `attorney_act_72_guard`,
`lawyer_or_authorized_signatory_handoff`, `esign_handoff_only`.

## Forbidden claims

- Contract validity, invalidity, enforceability, or legal risk is final.
- The clause must be amended in a particular way.
- Signing, sending, or electronic execution is safe.
- Counsel review is unnecessary.

## Memo-only implications

Use issue labels and questions. Escalate fact-specific legal judgment to counsel
or authorized company approvers.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when MOJ lawyer-act guidance, e-signature guidance, or platform rules
change.
