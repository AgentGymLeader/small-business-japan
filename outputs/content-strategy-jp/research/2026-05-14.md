# Research Dossier: content-strategy-jp

Research date: 2026-05-14
Output: `content-strategy-jp`

## Summary

Content strategy may propose drafts and evidence checklists. It must not become
claim substantiation, ad approval, stealth-marketing clearance, or publishing.

## Safe flags

`draft_only`, `evidence_required`, `ad_identification_check`,
`no_auto_publish`, `owner_review_required`.

## Forbidden claims

- No.1, cheapest, first, guaranteed sales improvement, public endorsement, or
  competitor superiority without evidence.
- PR labeling is unnecessary.
- Copy is legally safe to publish.

## Memo-only implications

Pair every objective claim with evidence, date, comparison basis, and owner
review status. Keep output as a draft package.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when CAA/JFTC advertising, stealth-marketing, or comparison guidance
changes.
