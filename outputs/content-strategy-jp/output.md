# Output Contract: content-strategy-jp

Status: proposed contract
Owner action boundary: read-only / draft-only / memo-only

## Mandatory output header

Every generated output must begin with:

```text
Small Business Japan by @agentgymleader. Community contribution; distribution is unofficial until upstream adoption.
Purpose: discussion memo for owner and, where needed, qualified professionals. Not advice, filing, submission, representation, final determination, or external sending.
Research checked: 2026-05-14. If this date is older than 180 days, treat legal/tax/labor/privacy/advertising review points as stale and refresh sources before use.
```

## Purpose

Prepare campaign, post, newsletter, or landing-page draft angles from sales and
customer signals. The output is a review memo and copy draft, not a public ad,
claim substantiation, or publishing workflow.

## Inputs

- Sales export, content brief, product notes, customer feedback, CRM export, or
  pasted campaign idea.
- Optional: prior claims evidence, survey data, comparison basis, influencer or
  affiliate plan, brand guide.

## Required output sections

1. `Summary`
2. `Audience / offer candidates`
3. `Draft copy angles`
4. `Claims requiring evidence`
5. `Advertising / PR display checks`
6. `Risk notes`
7. `Approval boundary`

## Japan compliance checks

- Mark performance, quality, price, comparison, No.1, testimonial, public-body,
  and influencer/affiliate claims as evidence-required.
- Require advertising identification where relevant.
- Separate internal strategy from publish-ready copy.

## Negative scope

Do not claim No.1, cheapest, first, guaranteed improvement, public endorsement,
PR-label unnecessary, or competitor superiority without stated evidence. Do not
publish, schedule, upload, or approve ad copy.

## Approval pause

Stop before posting, sending, uploading to ad platforms, giving influencers
instructions, or changing live LP/newsletter/social content.

## Output format

Markdown memo and draft copy only. Include an evidence checklist beside any
objective claim.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when advertising, stealth-marketing, comparative-advertising, or
platform review guidance changes.
