# Output Contract: design-tool-handoff-jp

Status: proposed contract
Owner action boundary: read-only / memo-only / checklist-only

## Mandatory output header

Every generated output must begin with:

```text
Small Business Japan by @agentgymleader. Community contribution; distribution is unofficial until upstream adoption.
Purpose: discussion memo for owner and, where needed, qualified professionals. Not advice, filing, submission, representation, final determination, or external sending.
Research checked: 2026-05-14. If this date is older than 180 days, treat legal/tax/labor/privacy/advertising review points as stale and refresh sources before use.
```

## Purpose

Convert approved content notes into a portable design handoff memo for Canva,
Figma, Slides, a designer, or the upstream Small Business design workflow. The
output is a memo and asset checklist, not a rights clearance or published
design.

## Inputs

- Approved content brief, brand notes, target format, product photos, logo file
  reference, template reference, or pasted design request.
- Optional: Canva/Figma/Slides destination, rights notes, font/asset license
  notes, ad platform requirements.

## Required output sections

1. `Summary`
2. `Design handoff memo`
3. `Asset checklist`
4. `Rights and license review points`
5. `Tool handoff notes`
6. `Approval boundary`

## Japan compliance checks

- Mark copyright, trademark, likeness, font, template, AI-generated asset,
  product-photo, and platform-review status as unconfirmed.
- Treat Canva or other tool availability as optional handoff, not a required
  connector.
- Keep source ownership and license notes visible.

## Negative scope

Do not claim commercial use is fully cleared, copyright is transferred,
trademark use is safe, Canva license is confirmed, social/ad review will pass,
or rights infringement is impossible. Do not publish or submit final designs.

## Approval pause

Stop before uploading to design tools, publishing, printing, submitting ads,
using third-party likenesses, or distributing templates.

## Output format

Markdown memo and asset checklist only. If an upstream Canva workflow exists,
treat it as an alias or handoff target with explicit owner approval.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when copyright, trademark, design-right, or relevant platform-license
guidance changes.
