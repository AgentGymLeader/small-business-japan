# Research Dossier: business-pulse-jp

Research date: 2026-05-14
Output: `business-pulse-jp`

## Summary

Business pulse outputs can summarize internal KPIs and public indicators, but
they must not overstate causality, forecast certainty, public-data meaning, or
professional advice.

## Safe flags

`memo_only`, `source_date_required`, `indicator_definition_required`,
`hypothesis_not_causation`, `professional_review_for_tax_labor_legal`.

## Forbidden claims

- A cause is determined.
- An action guarantees profit.
- A loan, subsidy, tax, or labor result is assured.
- Public statistics guarantee future performance.

## Memo-only implications

Use `observed`, `possible`, and `question for review`. Include source notes and
avoid deterministic language.
