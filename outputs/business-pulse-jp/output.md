# Output Contract: business-pulse-jp

Status: proposed contract
Owner action boundary: read-only / memo-only

## Mandatory output header

Every generated output must begin with:

```text
Small Business Japan by @agentgymleader. Community contribution; distribution is unofficial until upstream adoption.
Purpose: discussion memo for owner and, where needed, qualified professionals. Not advice, filing, submission, representation, final determination, or external sending.
目的: オーナーおよび必要に応じて担当士業と確認するための整理メモです。助言・申請・代行・最終判断・外部送信は行いません。
Research checked: 2026-05-14. If this date is older than 180 days, treat legal/tax/labor/privacy/advertising review points as stale and refresh sources before use.
```

## Purpose

Prepare a weekly, monthly, or quarterly owner brief from business records and
public indicators. The output highlights observations, hypotheses, decisions to
make, and professional-review questions. It does not promise outcomes or make
tax, financing, labor, or legal decisions.

## Inputs

- Accounting/sales export, invoice register, CRM export, calendar notes,
  customer pulse memo, public indicator note, or owner update.
- Optional: industry benchmark source, prior brief, board/advisor format.

## Required output sections

1. `Summary`
2. `Signals`
3. `Decisions to make`
4. `Risks / watch items`
5. `Professional review questions`
6. `Source notes`
7. `Approval boundary`

## Japan compliance checks

- Cite public statistics with source, publication date, access date, and
  definition where available.
- Treat DI and survey indicators as context, not forecasts or guarantees.
- Separate KPI observations from causal conclusions and professional advice.

## Negative scope

Do not claim a cause is determined, profit is guaranteed, a loan/subsidy/tax
advantage will be obtained, professional review is unnecessary, or official
statistics guarantee future performance. Do not send reports externally.

## Approval pause

Stop before sending briefs to lenders, investors, advisors, employees, or
external stakeholders; making financing/tax/labor/legal decisions; or writing
to systems.

## Output format

Markdown memo only. Use source notes for every external statistic or benchmark.

## Refresh trigger

Refresh when public-data terms, SME survey sources, or professional-boundary
guidance changes.
